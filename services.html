Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical Vaastu consultation take?

A typical residential consultation takes 2-3 hours, while commercial properties may require 3-5 hours depending on the size and complexity.

Do I need to make structural changes to my property?

Not necessarily. We prioritize non-structural solutions whenever possible. Most Vaastu corrections can be achieved through furniture rearrangement, color adjustments, and energy balancing remedies.

How soon can I expect results after implementing Vaastu changes?

Many clients report feeling an immediate shift in energy after implementing our recommendations. However, more tangible results typically manifest within 3-6 months as the positive energy fully establishes in your space.

Do you provide services outside your city?

Yes, we offer consultations nationwide. For clients outside our city, we charge additional travel expenses. We also provide online consultations based on floor plans and photographs for those who cannot arrange in-person visits.

Is Vaastu consultation necessary for apartment buildings?

Absolutely. While you may have limitations in changing the main structure, significant improvements can be made through internal arrangements, decor, and energy-balancing remedies that can transform your apartment's energy.
