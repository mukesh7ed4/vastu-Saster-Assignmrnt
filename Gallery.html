Featured Project: The Sharma Residence

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra

Project Type: Complete Residential Vaastu Correction

Challenge: The Sharma family was experiencing health issues, financial obstacles, and interpersonal conflicts. Their home had several Vaastu defects, including a southwest toilet, kitchen in the northeast, and a cluttered north zone.

Solution: Our comprehensive Vaastu correction included:

  • Relocating the kitchen to the southeast corner
  • Converting the northeast area into a meditation space
  • Repositioning beds in all bedrooms for optimal energy flow
  • Color correction in key areas
  • Strategic placement of Vaastu remedies

Result: Within three months, the family reported improved health, unexpected financial gains, and a more harmonious home environment. The project perfectly demonstrates how Vaastu corrections can transform lives.
