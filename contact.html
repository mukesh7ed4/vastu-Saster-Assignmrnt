Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vaastu consultation?

Vaastu consultation is an ancient Indian architectural science that aims to create harmony between humans and nature by designing spaces that promote positive energy flow and balance the five elements.

How long does a consultation take?

A typical residential consultation takes 2-3 hours, while commercial spaces may require 3-4 hours depending on the size and complexity.

Do I need to make structural changes to my property?

Not necessarily. Our approach focuses on practical and non-destructive remedies first. Structural changes are suggested only when absolutely necessary and after considering all alternatives.

How soon can I see results after implementing Vaastu changes?

Many clients report positive changes within 21-45 days of implementing our recommendations. However, results can vary based on the nature of the changes and individual circumstances.
